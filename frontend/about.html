💬 About

Welcome to FinanceLab — a space to explore the world of finance and economics in a clear, hands-on way.

Here, you’ll find simple explanations, real examples, and interactive tools designed to make complex ideas click. Whether you’re learning how interest grows, how markets find balance, or how businesses measure success, FinanceLab helps you see the numbers come to life.

Explore three main areas:

  • 💰 Personal Finance – budgeting, compound interest, and how loans really work.
  • 📈 Economics – experiment with supply and demand, production frontiers, and market trends.
  • 🏢 Business Finance – dive into financial statements, investments, and the effects of inflation.

FinanceLab was created by Samuel Soto, a student developer passionate about making financial literacy simple, practical, and engaging for everyone.
